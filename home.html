About coffee

Coffee is a beverage made from the roasted beans of the fruit. of the coffee tree. It is traditionally served hot, but can also be consumed ice cream. Coffee is a stimulant because it contains caffeine - usually 80 to 140 mg for every 207 ml depending on the method of preparation. Studies have shown that people who drink four cups of coffee a day have a lower risk of dying from a heart attack. Day 13/04 is World Coffee Day!
